How We Work

Our Process

A proven approach that takes your idea from concept to launch and beyond.

01

Discover

Every project begins with understanding. We learn your business, your target users, and your goals through structured research and discovery workshops. This phase defines the core problem your product needs to solve, maps out user journeys, and shapes the product vision that guides everything we build.

02

Design

Before writing any code, we bring your product to life visually. Our design phase includes wireframes, interactive prototypes, and polished UI design in Figma. We validate design decisions with user feedback so that what we build is grounded in real user needs, not assumptions.

03

Build

Development happens in focused agile sprints with regular demos and progress updates. We write clean, well-tested, scalable code using modern frameworks and best practices. You are always in the loop and can see working software at every stage of development.

04

Test

Quality assurance is built into every sprint, not bolted on at the end. We run rigorous automated testing, performance benchmarking, cross-browser and cross-device testing, and user acceptance testing to make sure your product works flawlessly before it reaches real users.

05

Launch

We handle deployment, infrastructure setup, monitoring, and launch-day support to make sure your product goes live smoothly. Whether deploying to cloud platforms like AWS and Vercel or submitting to the App Store and Google Play, we ensure everything performs at scale from day one.

06

Improve

Launch is just the beginning. We help you iterate on your product based on real user data, analytics, and feedback. Post-launch support includes bug fixes, performance optimisation, new feature development, and strategic product improvements that help you grow over time.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about working with FXiON and our software development services.

What services does FXiON offer?

FXiON offers a comprehensive range of software development services including web app development, mobile app development for iOS and Android, AI product development, MVP development for startups, UI/UX design, and business automation. We use modern technologies including React, Next.js, Flutter, Python, Node.js, and AI tools like OpenAI and LangChain to deliver production-ready digital products.

Where is FXiON based?

FXiON LTD is a software development company registered and operating in the United Kingdom. We work with clients across the UK and internationally, providing remote and collaborative development services for startups, founders, and growing businesses.

How long does it take to build an MVP?

FXiON typically delivers a minimum viable product within four to eight weeks, depending on the complexity and scope of the project. Our process starts with a focused discovery phase to define the core features that matter most, followed by rapid design and development sprints to get your product to market as quickly as possible without sacrificing quality.

What technologies does FXiON use?

FXiON works with a modern, battle-tested technology stack. For frontend development, we use React, Next.js, Vue, and TypeScript. Our backend services are built with Node.js, Python, REST APIs, and GraphQL. We use Flutter for cross-platform mobile apps, PostgreSQL and MongoDB for databases, AWS and Vercel for cloud infrastructure, and OpenAI, LangChain, and RAG systems for AI product development.

Does FXiON build AI-powered products?

Yes, AI product development is one of FXiON's core specialisations. We build AI-powered platforms, intelligent automation tools, and integrate machine learning capabilities into products using technologies like OpenAI, LangChain, vector embeddings, and retrieval-augmented generation systems. We focus on integrating AI where it creates genuine business value rather than using it as a superficial feature.

What is FXiON's development process?

FXiON follows a structured six-phase development process: Discover (research and problem definition), Design (wireframes, prototypes, and UI design), Build (agile development sprints with regular demos and updates), Test (quality assurance, performance, and user testing), Launch (deployment, monitoring, and go-live support), and Improve (post-launch iteration based on real user data and analytics).

Can FXiON build both web and mobile apps?

Yes, FXiON builds both web applications and mobile apps. For web, we use frameworks like React, Next.js, and Vue to create responsive, scalable platforms. For mobile, we use Flutter to build cross-platform apps that run natively on both iOS and Android from a single codebase. We can also develop unified products that span web and mobile with a consistent design and user experience across all platforms.

Does FXiON work with startups?

Yes, FXiON has a strong focus on working with startups and early-stage companies. We understand the pace and constraints of startup life — lean budgets, the need for fast pivots, and the urgency to validate ideas quickly. Our MVP development service is specifically designed to help founders go from concept to working product in weeks, not months, with a focus on core features that prove market fit.

Web App Development

Whether you need a SaaS dashboard, an internal tool, or a customer-facing platform, we engineer web applications that perform at scale and are built to grow with your business. We also provide ongoing maintenance, version updates, and feature enhancements to keep your mobile app competitive and aligned with evolving platform guidelines from Apple and Google.
