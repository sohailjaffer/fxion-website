## Where to add the assets

In Google Ads: open the campaign (or ad group) → **Assets** tab
(older accounts: "Ads & assets" → "Assets") → click **+** and choose
Sitelink / Callout / Structured snippet. Adding them at the **campaign**
level is usually best so they show across all ad groups.
